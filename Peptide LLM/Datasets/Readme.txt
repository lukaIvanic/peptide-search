OBJECTIVE / CILJ (project focus):
- Primary: build a structured dataset of **peptide sequences** and experimentally reported properties, especially **self-assembly / supramolecular hydrogels** (plus conditions like pH/concentration/temperature, morphology, validation methods, CAC/CGC/MGC when available).
- Secondary: other peptide labels also appear in the included datasets (e.g., catalytic activity, LLPS, antiviral activity).

Ovo su skupovi podataka koje sam sortirao prema važnosti od najvažnijeg:

1. Self-assembling peptides (42256_2024_928_MOESM3_ESM)
- popis peptida koji imaju self-assembly (prvi sheet), uz ekperimentalnu tehniku kojom je to potvrđeno, te oni koji ga nemaju (drugi sheet) i DOI za paper iz kojeg je izvučena informacija


2. Catalytic dataset (Peptides cat. func. prot. AA)
- nalazi se na: https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/6s9kxj2ndr/2
- katalitička funkcija je izražena kvantitativno u stupcu Kcat/KM (vrijednost 0 je bila za negativnu klasu)


3. Catalytic dataset (Peptides cat. func. non-prot. AA)
- nalazi se na: https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/6s9kxj2ndr/2

4. Liquid-liquid phase separation (LLPS peptides)
- popis peptida koji razdvajaju faze i link na paper
